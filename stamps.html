PROMPTS STAMPS INSPO HOME

These are notable or interesting stamps from different eras and different countries. Hover over one to learn more.

RED MERCURY - 1856

These Austrian stamps depicting Mercury were newspaper postage stamps. Each of these rare stamps are worth roughly $40k.

LIBERTY BELL FOREVER - 2007

This stamp marks the beginning of United States Postal Service's Forever Stamp program.

INVERTED JENNY - 1918

This misprinted stamp became highly collectible for its upside-down airplanes. Only 100 were printed.

WPA POSTERS - 2017

Work by artists in the Depression era created these poster designs celebrating the Works Progress Administration.

PACIFIC 97 - 1997

Known for being the first U.S. triangular stamps.

DINOSAURS - 1989

This stamp was controversial in the scientific community, for the depicted creatures were apatosauruses, not brontosauruses as labelled.
